YouTube Jam

Watch any YouTube video in perfect sync with friends.

How it works

  1. Paste any YouTube URL.
  2. We give you a shareable link.
  3. Everyone who opens the link plays, pauses, and seeks together — in real time.

Share URLs look like /watch?v=VIDEO_ID&session=SESSION_ID — the same shape as a real YouTube link, just on a different host.

Powered by Supabase Realtime (free & open source). No central server, no database — sync flows peer-to-peer through Supabase's broadcast channels.
